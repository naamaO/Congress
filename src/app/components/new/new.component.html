The series "Sources for the Study of Jewish Culture" is a series of critical annotated editions of a wide
range of sources in Jewish Studies: Philosophy, Halakha, Poetry and more - many of which are published for
the first time.It is published by the World Union of Jewish Studies and the Rabbi Moshe and Amalia
Rosen Foundation.The books are available in Hebrew only.


Jewish Studies is the annual journal of the World Union of Jewish Studies.
Articles published in Jewish Studies are also available on JStor. Submission instructions can be found here.


"Eshkolot" is a series of books published in association with Herzog College.
