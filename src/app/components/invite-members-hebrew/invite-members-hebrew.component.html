Session participants
Please add the names and email addresses of all
the particlpants in your proposed session,
including yourself, preferably in the order you
would like their papers to be presented at the congress.
All participants will receive notification
by email that you submitted their names as part of this session.
The lecturers will receive instructions and a
link to submit their individual lecture proposals.

In the order you would like to be presented at the congress
Please mark the button next to your name. This will identify you as the person who submits the session.



Submit a Session
1. A session title in English and Hebrew (if possible)
2. A session abstract (up to 250 words)
3. Complete the names and email addresses of all session participants including the lecturers and the session chairperson.
4. A session consists of four lectures or three lectures and response, in addition to a session chair. If you cannot find four particlpants and a chair, you may apply with three lectures on the condition that the academic committee may add a fourth lecture to the session.
